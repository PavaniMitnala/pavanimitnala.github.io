Centre Rules Subheading

Membership Rules:

Membership fees are non-refundable and non transferable. There is no pro-rating on any missed sessions or vacation dates. Fees shall be paid in-time as stipulated.

Footwear:

Non-Marking indoor-only badminton court shoes (to wear inside court premises) are required when playing on the court. Sandal, Running/ Jogging/ Hiking/ Spike shoes are not permitted. Wearing shoes from home/car is not permitted as this may bring dust/sand particles inside court area.

Shuttle Cocks:

The cost of shuttle cocks is as expense that has to be borne between players. Nylon shuttle cocks may be purchased from Apex Badminton Centre office.

Dress Code:

Appropriate badminton game clothing is advised. This will be enforced strictly, with powers to the Assistant to ask the players to vacate the courts for not following the same.

Keeping Courts Clean:

Only closed bottled drinking water is allowed on court, food and canned drinks are not permitted.

Crossing behind Courts:

It is a common courtesy and safety precaution, when passing behind courts to wait between courts until the point played in complete. Players should pass behind the court quickly and in single file close to the wall with their group so as to not disrupt the flow of play.

Personal Conduct:

Players shall not conduct purchase or subscribe to any commercial business activity inside premises or solicit other players for any reason.

Personal belongings:

Management is not responsible for any personal belongings & vehicles inside or at outside parking.

Others:

  • Each playing member has 1 hour time slot only per day.
  • 6 players are fixed for each hour per court.
  • All players should leave the court once their allotted time slot is over.
  • Players should take care of their belongings/vehicles. Management is not responsible for anything lost in the court/parking.
  • Players should bring their own shuttle cocks & Rackets.
  • Only Centre appointed coaches will coach the students.
  • Food packets & drinks are not allowed inside shuttle courts.
  • Smoking and consumption of Alcohol is prohibited inside the premises.
  • Members have to carry their respective ID Cards.
  • Dependents of Members are not entertained to use the court.
  • Suggestions or complaints should be written in the Register kept with the Assistant. No verbal complaints will be entertained. Apex Badminton Centre may change the rules and regulations without notice at any time.
