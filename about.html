About us

Badminton is quite simply one of the most awesome sports you can play. We, at Apex Badminton, welcome you to an unparalleled Badminton experience. We can boast that our club in Bachupally(Hyderabad) provides the best facilities for our members.

Apex Badminton Centre, set in Bachupally in Nizampet Panchayat approximately 5 Kms from JNTU Hyderabad. The spacious courts are made with high quality wooden flooring. The Academy aims at promoting badminton as fitness and fun activity in a club class environment.

The aim and objective of our club is to promote the sport for all age groups. We have plans to partner with various Schools, Clubs and community centers who are working mutually for development of Physical Education and Health among the sport lovers of all age group.
